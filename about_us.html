WHO WE ARE

Mr Price is a JSE- listed, omni-channel retailer

We are more focused on provides fashion-value products to their customers

We offer homeware, sportswear, clothing, footwear, financial services and telecoms.

The first Mr Price store was opened in Klerkdorp, by Laurie Chiappini.

Mr price has about 3 030 stores in total and2 771 stores in South Africa.

OUR HISTORY

Mr Price was founded in 1985 by Laurie Chiappini, who opened the first store in Klerksdorp, South Africa. The company quickly grew and expanded its product offerings to include clothing, footwear, and accessories.
